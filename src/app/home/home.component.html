This is page for mushroom lovers. You are welcome if you follow real mushroom lovers rules:

  1. Never litter in forests
  2. Give some space to other mushroom pickers. There is enough mushrooms for everyone
  3. Do not disturb the animals. Do not shout
  4. Do not kick or distoy inedible mushrooms. Animlas can eat some of them
  5. You can pick mushrooms, berries, but leave other plants. Some are under protection
  6. Better not go alone, if you don't know well area. You can get lost easily in the forest
  7. Prepare yourself: take basket, knife, telephone, spray against ticks and mosquitoes, proper cloths, hat
  8. After mushroom picking check if you didn't have any ticks with you
  9. And most important have fun & relax!
