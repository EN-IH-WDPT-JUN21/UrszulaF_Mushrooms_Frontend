  1. Never litter in forests.
  2. If you don't know the mushroom, don't take it.
  3. Do not destroy the mycelium. Cover the trail of the fungus with mulch, then it will not dry out.
  4. Give some space to other mushroom pickers. There is enough mushrooms for everyone.
  5. Do not disturb the animals. Do not shout.
  6. Do not kick or distoy inedible mushrooms. Animlas can eat some of them.
  7. You can pick mushrooms, berries, but leave other plants. Some are under protection.
  8. Better not go alone, if you don't know well area. You can get lost easily in the forest.
  9. Prepare yourself: take basket, knife, telephone, spray against ticks and mosquitoes, proper cloths & hat.
  10. After mushroom picking check if you didn't have any ticks on you.

Most important have fun & relax!
